Establishment

The original AIIMS in New Delhi was established in 1956. Multiple new AIIMS campuses have since been developed under the Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suruksha Yojna(PMSSY).Recognized for world-class research with hundreds of publications annually,the institutes are consistently ranked among the top medical colleges in Asia.

Core Objectives

  • To develop a high standard of medical education in India.
  • To train personal in all important health activity branches.
  • To achieve self-sufficiency in post-graduate medical education.
  • To provide affordable, high-quality, and specialized healthcare services to the public.
